MHelper

Open Live Demo

MHelper is a campus-side social community that promotes the intelligence exchange and collaboration among UM people with diverse backgrounds. It serves as an online platform for any UM people to seek for help on their tasks or provide help with their skills and knowledge. Although it is an online social community, MHelper highly values offline interaction among people to work on tasks together and develop real-life social network.

MHelper Homepage

MHelper is unique to provide a wide range of specialties, hence a wide range of tasks and skills. Therefore, Helpees can easily get access to the skillsets needed for their tasks, while Helpers can easily find tasks fit for their skillsets and interests. PHP, Apache and MYSQL are the basic web development technology used in this project. Knowie

Open Live Demo

Knowie is an application that allows Facebook users to challenge themselves on how well they know about their Facebook friends in two-answer quiz format. It utilizes the Graph API data provided by Facebook. Generally, the application can be used by any Facebook users who have friends in their network. 

Game Homepage

The main purpose of the application is to allow the audience to test their knowledge on information of Facebook friends in a simple gameplay format. The application offers an alternative way for Facebook users to learn about their friends in the social network site in a gamification environment rather than the normal profile browsing activity. We would like to enable the audience to discover more about their friends and have fun in a quiz gameplay environment. We have implemented the scoring and ranking system as part of the gamification elements in order to add motivation for users to continue using the application.

Wrong Answer

This game requires users login with their facebook account. Since every question and fake answer is grabbed from Facebook API. The game is based on the php and mysql to randomly choose different kind of information from users' account. The game also provides easy ranking function to let users compare their 'familiarity' with their friends.
